Mouse Cursor History

This presentation is on the history of the mouse cursor.


SketchPad

The SketchPad introduced the idea of an onscreen avatar via a light pen far before the computer mouse.




Mac OS Classic

The design of Mac OS informs much of how we think of mouse cursors today. Their limited resolution, effected their aesthetic representation.




Cursor States

Mouse cursors have hover states, and change contextually as a cue to how you can interact with them.




Platform Specific

Cursors are platform specific and in some cases can be customized. One of many subtle indicators of an interface and their differences.




Mac OSX Beach Ball

As screen resolution increased, and graphic techniques changed. Increasing detail went in to the design of mouse cursors, and vector graphics allows them to scale to any size.
